About

About

This is the official profile page of Rafael Mugi Suzuki (鈴木ラファエル麦). While studying medicine at Tohoku University, he has worked across bioinformatics, systems biology, neuroscience, and machine-learning-supported research. This page serves as an entry point to research, projects, publications, and past academic activities.

The name also appears in Japanese as 鈴木ラファエル麦, and sometimes as 鈴木ラファエル or 鈴木ラファエルムギ. The site documents a conference presentation at the Japan Neuroscience Society, research experience at Georgia Tech and RWTH Aachen University, entrepreneurship programs, and business competitions.
